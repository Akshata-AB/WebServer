Families

  1. Home
  2. Families

Welcome parents and families! At Santa Clara, we provide a range of resources with the hope that you'll become active members of our community. We truly believe family involvement is important to ensuring your student has a rewarding and fulfilling college experience. It's our goal to keep you well-informed and connected, and we invite you to partner with us in your student's journey!

Parent & Family Programs

Whether it's at orientation, welcome weekend, family weekend, or commencement, there are many opportunities for parents and families to get involved in the Santa Clara experience.
